What LandwiseAI Is Today

An AI title-verification workbench for legal advisors and banks

Currently scoped to Tamil Nadu, the platform turns raw Encumbrance Certificates and Sale Deeds into a chain-of-title hierarchy, a 0–100 risk score, and a sign-able 9-section legal opinion — work that traditionally takes a senior advocate 2–6 weeks per parcel.

Document Intelligence

Gemini-vision OCR + LLM extraction handles bilingual Tamil-English ECs and Sale Deeds. Auto-detects discrepancies in extent, consideration, claimant name, and survey number across the EC ↔ deed pair.

Chain of Title (ReactFlow)

Reconstructs a 30-year ownership hierarchy from 397+ EC transactions in seconds. Visualises mortgages, releases, partitions, and POAs; flags encumbrance gaps over 3 years.

AI Risk Score

0–100 scoring with A–F grade, AI summary, and factor breakdown (encumbrance gaps, lis pendens, restricted-land flags, doc trustability). Cached in DB so the LLM runs once per audit, not per page-load.

Structured Legal Opinion

9-section draft: Possession, Land Nature, TN Land Reforms, Title Flow, Specific Protections, Acquisitions, Lis Pendens, Documents Checklist, Final Verdict. Markdown + branded PDF + digital signing.

Per-Deed Chat (RAG)

Lawyer can ask in natural language: "Was the discharge for mortgage 254/2011 registered in the same sub-registry?" — answers grounded in the specific PDF.

PDF Vault & Batch Audit

Categorised vault (EC / Sale Deed / Patta / POA), green/red/yellow status colors, audit-trail timeline, and multi-parcel batch mode for industrial-land aggregators.

Strategic Bets · Ranked by Leverage

10 moves that change the trajectory

These aren't generic SaaS advice. Each is a specific, India-property-specific bet against a named competitor or unmet need, ordered by impact-to-effort ratio.

1

Plug into Bank LOS / RBI Unified Lending Interface (ULI) Highest revenue 12-month build

Square Yards entered via RBIH; the second-mover slot is open. Sell title-health-score as an API priced ₹1,500–3,000 per file. Captures the ₹1,250–5,000 Cr/yr bank vetting pool. Start with 2–3 PSU + private bank pilots.

Distribution moat Competes with: Square Yards, Teal
2

Direct fetch from tnreginet.gov.in EC + Patta-Chitta + Adangal Removes friction

Eliminate manual upload — the #1 reason banks won't ship a self-serve flow. Add Karnataka Bhoomi, Telangana Dharani, AP Webland next. These four states cover ~40% of South Indian property volume. Partner with mypatta or build in-house.

Foundational Competes with: Landeed
3

Embedded title-insurance distribution (HDFC ERGO / Tata AIG) Open lane Margin engine

IRDAI-approved but distribution-starved. Bundle a ₹5K–15K title-insurance premium on every A/B-grade opinion; LandwiseAI takes 15–25% commission. Converts software ARR into per-transaction take-rate revenue.

Net-new monetisation No current player here
4

Lis Pendens automation via eCourts / NJDG court-feed Defensible moat

The weakest of the 9-section opinion today. A live court-feed integration (Provakil-style) turns it from "advocate calls the court" to "data refreshes nightly." No marketplace player can replicate this; it's a moat against PropClap and NoBroker.

Competes with: Provakil licensing
5

Batch-audit SaaS tier for industrial-land aggregators / SEZ developers High ACV

100+ parcel deals priced at ₹5–25 lakh/yr seat licence. This is where Teal makes most of its enterprise revenue. Targets the same DD heads at Adani, Reliance, Tata Realty, JSW Infrastructure who already need this monthly.

Enterprise contract Competes with: Teal enterprise
6

Advocate-marketplace overlay for B/C/D-grade opinions Convert tail to revenue

When the AI Title Health Score < 70, route to a panel advocate for ₹7,500–15,000 with revenue share. Converts the failure mode (LandwiseAI says "this title is risky, talk to a lawyer") into a monetised hand-off rather than a dead-end.

Hybrid: AI + advocate panel
7

WhatsApp-first vernacular status updates for tier-2 TN buyers Cheap to build B2C funnel

Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, Salem buyers transact via informal advocates and WhatsApp. A Tamil status bot (audit started → score ready → opinion signed) beats a web dashboard. Becomes a top-of-funnel for individual buyers paying ₹4,999.

8

Tamil → English deed translation as a paid add-on (~₹500/deed) Quick win

Captures NRI buyers (Singapore / US Tamil diaspora) who today pay advocates ₹5K+ for the same. The model already does it internally; expose it as a standalone product. ~₹5–10 lakh ARR with no incremental compute cost.

9

RERA-project verification module via Surepass API New segment

Targets the under-construction segment Landeed doesn't cover well. Pull builder default history, RERA filing status, and complaint count per project. Bundle into an existing legal opinion or sell standalone at ₹999 per project to retail buyers.

10

Free encumbrance-fee & stamp-duty calculator (top-of-funnel) Lead-gen

Use TN guideline values (revised Jul 2024) to auto-pre-fill stamp duty and registration fees. A free tool that captures email + phone, then upsells to the legal opinion. Same playbook ClearTax used to dominate ITR.

For Banks & HFCs

  • 5–10× faster turnaround on legal opinion (hours vs 2 weeks) shrinks loan-disbursal time directly
  • ₹3K–17K saved per file on legal vetting fees, scaling at ₹2K API price
  • Audit-trail for regulator — every flag, gap, mismatch logged with timestamps and reasoning
  • NPA reduction from catching defective titles before disbursal — relevant to RBI's Asset Quality Reviews

For Legal Advisors

  • 4–5× productivity per senior advocate (3–4 opinions/week → 15–20)
  • Same fee, lower effort — opinions still bill at ₹25K–50K but draft in 30 min
  • Defensible work product — every clause cites the source deed page, ready for digital signing
  • NRI segment unlock — Tamil-to-English translation makes diaspora due diligence trivial

For Retail Buyers

  • ₹4,999–6,999 self-serve opinion vs ₹15K–50K traditional advocate route
  • WhatsApp status updates in Tamil — accessible to first-time buyers in tier-2/3 cities
  • Title insurance bundling — concrete recourse if defect surfaces post-purchase
  • Pre-purchase clarity — direct attack on the 66% of civil litigation that's land-related

For Industrial Land Aggregators

  • ₹5–25 lakh/yr enterprise tier with batch audit on 100+ parcels
  • Project-feasibility days, not months — chain-of-title gaps surface immediately, not after legal review
  • SEZ / industrial-park use case — Adani Ports, Tata Realty, Reliance Industries all do this monthly
  • Risk dashboards for portfolio-level due diligence across geographies
